THE BURNING CARDS FESTIVAL 2017

Step 1

Get all the cards from the "DONE" column of the board along with the ones in the done-cards-box.

Step 2

Get along beside of Tamedia building, near to the smoking area where the Bicycles can be seen, in a friday at 18h - When the spirits are ready to receive our offers.

Step 3

Place a picture of our Lord Francesco and pour 3 sips of beer in front of his image for his honour and glory. In this moment all the cards must be unite in a recipient and placed on top of the poured beer.

Step 4

The ceremony master sets the cards on fire. The crowd must dance around the bonfire along with the drumming (drumming performed by volunteer participants).

Step 5

The smoke will reach the skies and annouce a new age of the board.
